Second Trimester

Often called the "honeymoon phase" journey

Weeks 13-26

Important Tips

  • 1 Continue prenatal vitamins
  • 2 Start exercises like prenatal yoga
  • 3 Begin planning for baby's arrival
  • 4 Consider maternity clothes
  • 5 Connect with your baby through gentle touch

What to Expect

  • 1 Energy levels increase
  • 2 Baby bump becomes visible
  • 3 Feel baby's first movements
  • 4 Reduced nausea
  • 5 Skin and hair changes
