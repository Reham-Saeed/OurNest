First Trimester

the beginning of your pregnancy journey

Weeks 1-12

Important Tips

  • 1 Take prenatal vitamins with folic acid
  • 2 Schedule your first prenatal appointment
  • 3 Stay hydrated and eat small, frequent meals
  • 4 Get plenty of rest fatigue is common
  • 5 Avoid alcohol, smoking, and raw foods

What to Expect

  • 1 Morning sickness may begin
  • 2 Breast tenderness
  • 3 Frequent urination
  • 4 Mood swing
  • 5 Food cravings or aversions
